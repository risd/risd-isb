The possibilities are huge. In a world where the pace of change is accelerating, the field of illustration is no exception. In fact, since the turn of the century, the traditional profession has been morphing at breathtaking speed. At RISD these changes are apparent not only in the tools of technology we use, but in the way we think, conceive of ourselves as makers and collaborate in the creation of visual communication.
 
Now, RISD Illustration is addressing the need for a new home for its dynamic, ever-evolving program through the 
total renovation of the Illustration Studies Building.
